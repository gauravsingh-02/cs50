Looking at someone’s top 100 songs by averaging energy, valence, and danceability gives a rough idea of their overall vibe—like “energetic and upbeat” or “calm and mellow.” But simply averaging can miss nuances; for example, it might hide strong mood swings in their music taste. A better approach would be to group songs by mood or use machine learning to spot patterns, capturing a fuller and more accurate picture of their musical personality.
